PEP — What you must know
72h
0 – 72 Hours — Act immediately
Go to the nearest clinic now. PEP must start within 72 hours of exposure to work.
28d
Days 1–28 — Take every day
Take PEP daily for 28 days without skipping. Free at all government clinics.
45d
Day 45 — Follow-up test
Return for an HIV test. If taken correctly PEP is over 99% effective.

PEP Emergency Guide
PEP must start within 72 hours of HIV exposure to be effective.
1
Call 1190 now. NACC and LVCT Health will direct you to the nearest PEP clinic. The call is free and confidential.
2
Go to any public hospital. Kenyatta National Hospital, Mbagathi, Pumwani, and Homa Bay County Hospital all provide PEP at any hour.
3
Say: I need PEP. You do not need to explain everything. The healthcare worker is there to help you.
4
Complete all 28 days. PEP only works when you finish the full course. It is free at government facilities.
